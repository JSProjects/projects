Requesting your Entitlement Key-Three Steps

An Entitlement Key is required to onboard your Free AP onto HiveManager NG.

Step 1) Please complete the form to the right and click submit.

Step 2) You will receive an email with your entitlement key.

  • Each device will be assigned one entitlement key that
    is valid for 3 years.

Step 3) Once your Entitlement Key is generated, you can apply the entitlement key by clicking on the gear icon in the upper right corner.

If you should have questions, or require assistance please send an email to: freeaps@aerohive.com.

Note: your entitlement key will generate within 20 minutes or less.
